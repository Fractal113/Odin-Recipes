Spaghetti

About the dish:

This dish is great for the family or a get together! Follow these simple steps for an amazing dish!It's traditional but also something fresh in its own right.I hope you enjoy it as much as I do! My family begs me to make this dish every holiday or event!!

What I enjoy most about this dish is how simple it is.A few things the carrots,sugar and onion make a huge difference.Adding pork is only optional. You can use a spicy italian sausage or any other kind of pork or sausage. The simple blend of seasonings bring out the traditional taste in the dish.It also is a dish that you can easily make before a family dinner or holiday dinner.The only time consuming step is the sauce but patience the longer the better. The carrots and onion add lots of depth while the sugar adds sweetness and brightens the dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 small onion finely chooped
  • 3 carrots peeled and choopppped
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound pork
  • 1 1/2 pound tomatoes
  • 6 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 pound spaghetti noodles

Cooking steps

  1. Heat oil on medium heat in a large, deep skillet. Add onion, carrots, and garlic and saut√© for 1 minute. Add ground beef and pork and continue to cook.
  2. When meat is browned and onions are soft, add tomatoes.
  3. When tomatoes soften and release their juices.Add sugar,salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer for six hours stir frequently.Add beef broth to thin sauce as needed.
  5. In a large pot cook spaghetti in salted water
  6. Serve and enjoy!
