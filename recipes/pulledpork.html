Pulled Pork

About the dish:

A great very easy crock pot dish that is also delicous everytime! Love making this recipe in the winter and summer!Great for any time of the year. Wondeful for sandwiches at the cook out or tacos for dinner!Very flavorful but still sweet while also scratching that bbq craving. My favorite thing about this dish is the servings! you can feed alot of people with this recipe! You'll know its time if the prok shreds very easily with a fork!

Ingredients

  • mustard
  • bbq sauce
  • garlic
  • Onion
  • 4lbs pork shoulder
  • chicken broth
  • Thyme

Cooking steps

  1. Put one teaspoon of oil in bottom of crock pot
  2. Place pork in pot atop of oil then begin to add liquid ingredients
  3. Next add onion,garlic and thyme
  4. Cook for 6 to 7 hours.Or until pork falls apart easily
  5. Serve on buns or taco chells of your choice.Reheat as you please!
